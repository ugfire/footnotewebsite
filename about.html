Footnote Consults Ltd.

FCL is constituted by smart hardworking and resilient engineers, architects and project managers adaptive to the revolving technology and innovation to provide client-centered specific satisfaction. FCL majors in Engineering Consultancy and project management. However, we partner with Delanne Group Ltd and Bluecore Uganda Limited as our reliable affiliates in Real estate, Credit/ Finance and Supplies and Construction of Civil works. Over the years, we have accomplished a number of residential and commercial buildings and apartments within Kampala and Wakiso under design and build arrangements, designs and design reviews for roads and drainage structures within Kampala and Wakiso, Civil and Electrical works/ services/ supplies for High Voltage – Substations in Uganda and Supply of building material and furniture all over the country. We strive to harness a hybrid of efficient and effective technology to always serve our esteemed clientele base. We assure you of a client-centered service and we thank you for choosing us.

Our Services

Our Philosophy

To provide precise, personalized civil engineering services and products that satisfy clients’ needs by leveraging highly skilled staff, strong relationships, and cost-effective solutions in an atmosphere that fosters collaboration and creativity.

Our Mission

Be a competent engineering, construction and project management company by achieving extraordinary results for our customers, building satisfying careers for our people, and earning a fair return on the value we deliver.

Our Vision

Footnote Consults Limited, FCL is a privately owned company that was established by Ugandans in January 2018. The Company offers a broad spectrum of services in Civil Engineering, Architecture and Project management.
